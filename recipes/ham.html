Odins' Favorite Baked Ham

Description

This is one of the best Baked Hams that you'll ever try. This recipe is not just a favorite of a god, but will also be a holiday staple in your own home. Please enjoy Odins' Favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 pound) - Bone-in Ham, Rump Portion
  • 1/2 Cup - Whole Cloves
  • 1 Cup - Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 4 Cups - Water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place ham in a large roasting pan; press cloves into the top at 1- to 2-inch intervals.

  2. Step 2

    Pack the top with a layer of brown sugar.

  3. Step 3

    Pour enough water into the bottom of the roasting pan to come to a 1-inch depth; cover the pan tightly with aluminum foil.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in the preheated oven for 4 1/2 to 5 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center, making sure it is not touching the bone, should read at least 160 degrees F (72 degrees C).

  5. Step 5

    Let ham rest for about 20 minutes before carving.
